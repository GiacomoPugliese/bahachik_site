Understanding Bahachik's Special Formula

Bahachik's formula harnesses the power of natural ingredients to offer exceptional hair care. From moisturizing Shea Butter to gentle surfactants, our blend prioritizes hair health and sustainability.

Shea Butter

Known for its rich moisturizing properties, Shea Butter promotes healthy hair growth and offers soothing benefits in pet care.

Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate (SCI)

A coconut oil-derived surfactant, SCI provides gentle cleansing and produces a creamy lather without stripping natural oils.

Allantoin

Found in the comfrey plant, Allantoin soothes the skin and promotes wound healing.

Calcium Lactate

Derived from dairy products, it acts as a moisturizer and light exfoliant for the scalp, balancing pH levels.

Disodium Laurylglucosides Hydroxypropyl Citrate

A biodegradable surfactant known for irritation reduction and naturally derived benefits.

Guar Hydroxypropyltrimonium Chloride

Derived from guar beans, it conditions hair, offers anti-static properties, and moisturizes the skin.

Citric Acid

An exfoliant from citrus fruits, Citric Acid also adjusts the pH of formulations for smoother skin.

Caprylyl Glycol

A versatile ingredient, it softens skin and hair, also acting as a preservative.

Sodium PCA

A natural skin-identical ingredient, Sodium PCA retains water ensuring skin and hair hydration.

Behentrimonium Methosulfate & Cetearyl Alcohol

A combination that offers conditioning for hair, reduces static electricity, and imparts softness.
